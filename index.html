About Paytra

Hi! Paytra here — your friendly local fitness coach! My philosophy is that fitness should be FUN! I credit my high energy and pep-talk-filled workouts with creating a healthy and motivational environment for my clients.

I am also a professional performer, dancer, and singer— and that truly has shaped my training practice and approach. As someone who constantly needs to be at peak performance, I thrive with helping my clients find confidence and drive in their daily lives.

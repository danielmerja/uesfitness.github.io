About Paytra

Hi! Paytra here — your friendly "pocket" fitness coach *wink wink* I decided to start my virtual training/nutrition business after 10 years of working with so many incredible women who were professionals, moms, and/or entrepreneurs. I realized that time and money is usually the #1 reason people don't stick to a fitness routine. Thats why I'm here — to be your on call accountability parter, trainer, and hype girl both in and out of the gym. I'm so passionate about helping other women feel and look their best!

My philosophy is that fitness should be FUN, ACCESSIBLE, and AFFORDABLE. I credit my high energy and pep-talk-filled workouts with creating a healthy and motivational environment for my clients. My coaching style is relaxed, confident, and challenging— so you'll be laughing and smiling while also cursing me for making you do another set of burpees. I believe in being available to my clients… my work doesn't stop during our session. I'm always a text or call away about any fitness or nutrition related questions. Plus— virtual training and coaching allows for competitive prices and flexibility on time!

As a professional musician, dancer, and artist— I think my love for entertaining and empowerment fuels my passion for using fitness as a way to change lives. I'm there with my clients through all the phases of life, making sure they stay on track in their fitness journey and continue to make time for themselves.

Whether you're training for a marathon, want to correct a lower back injury through exercise, have a teen or child who needs a little extra push, or simply want a kick-ass workout while keeping it fun — I'm here for you!

FAQ

How does virtual training work?

All you need is an iPhone or computer camera. Many of my clients have no equipment at all. Some have basic weights or bands. Others take me to the gym with them virtually! We'll make anything work to reach your goals.

How long are sessions?

45 minutes to 1 hour

Cancellation policy?

Sessions canceled within 24 hours will be charged in full.

I understand life happens, so I always give clients one "free" cancellation of less than 24 hour... anything after that is charged.

Do you offer any flexibility in pricing for virtual or other unique cases?

Yes— I am always willing to create custom packages for special cases. Please contact me so we can work something out!
